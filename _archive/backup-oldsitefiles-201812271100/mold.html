All registration for our classes is rolling which means you can join whenever you want and get the same step-by-step personal instruction that we provide to every student.

Beginning Mold Making

Instructors: TBD
Thursday 7-10pm
$285 for 10 weeks or $400 for 16 weeks

In this class students will learn basic techniques of plaster molding. We will cover how to make one piece molds, two piece molds, waste molds in plaster, and latex rubber molds.

Intermediate Mold Making

Instructors: TBD
Thursday 7-10pm
$285 for 10 weeks or $400 for 16 weeks

Students in this class will learn more advanced molding techniques. Making multiple piece plaster molds, urethane and silicones rubber molds, techniques for drawing out and gageing symmetrical forms in plaster using a template, as well as model building in plaster and Styrofoam will be covered.
